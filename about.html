About Us (Hamare Baare Mein)

Namaste! Hamari website Ayart Studio par aapka swagat hai. Hum Rudrapur (Udham Singh Nagar, Uttarakhand) ke rehne wale Class 8 ke students hain.

Mera naam Arnav Tyagi hai, main ek artist hoon aur mujhe 1 saal se zyada ka sketching experience hai. Mere sath mere dost Yugank Vishnoi hain, jo ek beginner artist aur web designer hain. Hum dono ne milkar is website ko design kiya hai.

Hamara Maksad: Aajkal internet par art sikhane ke liye bohot mushkil tareeke bataye jaate hain. Hum yahan bacchon aur beginners ke liye ekdam aasan, step-by-step drawing guides aur tutorials share karte hain taaki koi bhi aasani se art seekh sake.
